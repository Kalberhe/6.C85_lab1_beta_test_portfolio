DSC 80 Final Project: Predicting League of Legends Match Outcomes

This project uses data from professional League of Legends matches to predict match outcomes based on early-game statistics (e.g. first tower, first dragon, kills, assists). I performed exploratory data analysis, hypothesis testing, logistic regression and gradient boosting models, and also conducted fairness audits to inspect side-based bias. The final model achieved over 95% accuracy and revealed subtle bias favoring the blue side.
